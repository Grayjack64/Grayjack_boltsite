Grayjack Holdings

Three Decades of Innovation in Telecommunications, Logistics, and Software Development

Our History

Mid-1990s

The Beginning: Natel Networks

Danny Bannister pioneered one of the world's first carrier-grade VoIP networks through Natel Networks. This groundbreaking venture helped establish the foundation for modern internet telecommunications.

Late 1990s - 2004

Explosive Growth

At its peak, Natel Networks transported nearly 1 billion minutes of traffic per month, operating across 48 US cities and 7 countries. The company became a major player in the global telecommunications infrastructure.

2004

Strategic Exit & New Beginnings

Following the successful sale of Natel Networks, Danny Bannister founded Grayjack Holdings as a technology investment and development vehicle. Named after his twin sons Grayson and Jackson, the company was established to hold interests in innovative ventures and continue pushing technological boundaries.

2000s

Telecommunications Innovation

Grayjack Holdings developed cutting-edge technology for the telecommunications industry, serving over 200 Competitive Local Exchange Carriers (CLECs) across the United States. The company created sophisticated Class 4 and Class 5 switching software for Cisco Systems under the Red River Networks brand, solidifying its position as a key technology provider in the telecom sector.

2010s

Diversification into Logistics

Recognizing opportunities beyond telecommunications, Grayjack Holdings expanded into the logistics sector with the acquisition of Westech Logistics, an Oklahoma-based trucking company. This was followed by the establishment of Red River Distribution, a Dallas-area third-party logistics provider powered by proprietary software developed in-house.

2020s

Software Innovation Era

Grayjack Holdings accelerated its focus on software and application development, creating a diverse portfolio of innovative solutions spanning health and wellness, fashion technology, agricultural management, pet care, and media streaming. The company established Red River Labs as its technology innovation arm, developing AI-powered applications and cutting-edge digital solutions.

About Our Founder

Danny Bannister is a telecommunications pioneer and technology entrepreneur with over three decades of experience building transformative companies. From creating one of the world's first carrier-grade VoIP networks to developing enterprise software solutions serving hundreds of businesses, Danny has consistently identified emerging opportunities and built successful ventures. His vision continues to guide Grayjack Holdings as it innovates across multiple industries.
